Get in Touch with SportSurge

At SportSurge, we are committed to maintaining an open line of communication with our community. We believe that transparency and responsiveness are essential to building a trusted sports streaming directory. Whether you are a user experiencing technical difficulties, a copyright holder with a legal inquiry, or a potential partner, we encourage you to contact us.

Our administrative team reviews incoming messages daily and strives to address every legitimate query with clarity and efficiency.

Official Contact Method

To ensure your inquiry reaches the correct department and is processed securely, all official communication must be directed to our designated email address:

Email: admin@sportsurge.fit

Please provide a clear subject line and detailed information regarding your request to help us assist you faster.

Types of Inquiries We Handle

You may contact the SportSurge team regarding the following matters:

  • Technical Support: Reporting broken links, site errors, or compatibility issues with specific browsers or devices.
  • General Inquiries: Questions about how to navigate the schedule or use the platform features.
  • Legal & DMCA Compliance: Submitting formal copyright notices or counter-notifications in accordance with our DMCA Policy.
  • Business & Advertising: Proposals for partnerships or advertising opportunities on sportsurge.fit.
  • Feedback: Suggestions on how we can improve the SportSurge experience for the global sports community.

Note: For copyright-related matters, please review our DMCA Policy page first to ensure your notice contains all legally required elements.

Response Standards

We aim to respond to all valid inquiries within 2–5 business days.

Due to the high volume of traffic SportSurge receives during major sporting events, response times may vary. We ask that you avoid sending duplicate emails regarding the same issue, as this may delay our ability to process your request.

Important Operational Notice

It is important to remember that SportSurge operates strictly as an informational indexing service.

We do not host, upload, store, or broadcast video content on our servers. All streams found on our platform are hosted by third-party websites. Consequently, we cannot resolve issues related to the content ownership, video quality, or buffering of specific third-party streams. Such concerns should be directed to the website actually hosting the content.

Communication Channels

SportSurge does not offer support via social media, instant messaging apps, or community forums. The email address listed above is the only official channel for contacting the SportSurge administration.

Thank you for your support and for making SportSurge your preferred destination for live sports information.
